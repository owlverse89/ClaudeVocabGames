Sort-a-Suffix

Words fall like leaves — add the suffix and sort them!

How to Play

  1. 1
    Words drift down on falling leaves. Each word is a base word — your job is to figure out which suffix turns it into a real word!
  2. 2
    Three barrels sit at the bottom, each labelled with a suffix: -ful, -ous, or -ish.
  3. 3
    Click a leaf to select it — it glows gold and pauses mid-air. Then click the right barrel to sort it!
  4. 4
    For example: "wonder" + -ful = wonderful ✓  |  "child" + -ish = childish ✓
  5. 5
    Correct sort = +10 pts. Wrong barrel or word hits the ground = −5 pts. Good luck!
