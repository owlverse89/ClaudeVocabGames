How to Play 📖
  • 👆
    Flip a Card

    Click any face-down card to reveal the hidden word underneath.

  • 🔍
    Hunt Its Synonym

    Now flip a second card. If it means the same thing — it's a match! Both cards stay open and light up gold.

  • 🔄
    No Match? Remember & Retry

    If they don't match, the cards flip back over. Remember where they were — it'll help next time!

  • 🏆
    Match All 6 Pairs to Win!

    Uncover every synonym pair on the board and celebrate your win.

💡 Tip: Take your time — there's no timer. Focus on remembering where you've seen each word!
